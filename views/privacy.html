Privacy Policy

Last updated June 25th, 2025

At DividendsSpot, we value your privacy and are committed to protecting your personal data. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and protect your information when you visit our website.

Information We Collect

We do not collect any personally identifiable information on our users.

Analytics Tracking

We use a privacy-focused third-party analytics software to understand how our website is used. This helps us improve the site and identify popular sections. This software collects only aggregated, anonymized data that does not personally identify you. We do not track your individual activity across the web or link it to any personal information.

Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Any changes will be posted on this page, and the "last updated" date at the top will reflect the changes. We encourage you to review this page periodically to stay informed about how we protect your information.
